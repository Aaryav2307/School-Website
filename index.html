Campus Life

School yoga room

Was established in 2017

Kothari International School hosted a Yoga session for the staff members. The resource person focused on the flexibility of body and mental peace. Mr. Sandeep professed that yoga is accepted worldwide as one of the most effective form of exercise to influence the mental control. A qualified yoga trainer imparted basic yoga knowledge to the staff members. The staff members practiced various yoga asanas under the guidance of the trainer, starting with warming up and stretching followed by the series of asanas. A yoga capsule was also designed for the students of the school which has to be done every day. The capsule consisted of some asanas and mantras which will help the students and teachers to attain mental peace and healthy growth All the staff members really enjoyed the session and could feel a difference in their bodies.

School ICT Lab

Was established in 2000

The fully networked ICT laboratories are equipped with the latest computers and other state of the art IT applications, including interactive learning tools and also offer Internet connectivity. Our computers are all high grade TFT screen units with ancillary printers and are carefully monitired. Each child has his own work-station and access to an individual computer for hands-on practice.Our teaching aids are also consistently upgraded to facilitate awareness and knowledge.
